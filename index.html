Artist Profile

Lemrish John Belarmino, also known online as Lem_milky, is a passionate visual storyteller who bridges the gap between traditional and digital art. Whether it's ballpoint sketches or vibrant digital illustrations, Lemrish brings each character to life with emotion, detail, and motion.

Starting with stickman storyboards scribbled in old notebooks, he found his voice through character design and anime fanart. His black-and-white line art often carries a raw, honest energy—while his digital works glow with color, depth, and storytelling. Today, Lemrish continues to explore animation, recently completing an internship at Rocketsheep Studio, where he animated background characters in studio productions.

He’s currently focused on mastering anatomy, deepening his animation skills, and building a portfolio that reflects his growth and ambition. For Lemrish, art isn’t just a passion—it’s a journey of curiosity and motion, driven by the question: “What if this drawing could move?”
